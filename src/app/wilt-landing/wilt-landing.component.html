What is WILT ü§î???

WILT is the acronym for What I Learned Today. We call it like this because this is simple and we love it.

Most of us have our own way of remembering things that we learn or encounter during work, travel, reading books, watching movies etc. Some people prefer to write down those things in their notes or in their daily diaries. Some people are confident enough to memorize those.

WILT brings a platform to you and your ideas, leanings and all those one or two liners which you learn, listen, read, encounter during any time of your day
